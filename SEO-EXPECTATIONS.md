## Why You Don't Rank Yet

### 1. **Google Hasn't Crawled Your Site Yet**

Even though your fix is live, Google doesn't know about it until:
- ✅ Googlebot crawls your site (can take 1-7 days)
- ✅ Google indexes the new content (can take 1-4 weeks)
- ✅ Google evaluates your site's quality (ongoing)
- ✅ Google assigns rankings (can take 2-8 weeks)

**Timeline:** Minimum 2 weeks before you see ANY organic traffic

---

### 2. **"Bókhald" is EXTREMELY Competitive**

Think about it:
- 🏢 Hundreds of accounting firms in Iceland
- 🏢 Many have websites that are 5-10+ years old
- 🏢 They have thousands of backlinks
- 🏢 They rank for "bókhald" because of domain authority

**Your site:**
- 🆕 Just launched (or just got SEO fixed)
- 🆕 Zero backlinks (probably)
- 🆕 No domain authority yet
- 🆕 No historical data

**Analogy:** You're a new restaurant trying to rank #1 for "restaurant" when Gordon Ramsay's place has been there for 20 years!

---

### 3. **Google Doesn't Trust New Sites Yet**

Google uses the "Sandbox Effect" for new sites:
- New domains get lower rankings for 2-6 months
- Google wants to see if your site is legitimate
- Google wants to see consistent content
- Google wants to see real user engagement

**This is normal and happens to EVERYONE!**

## How to Track Progress

### Google Search Console (Check weekly)

1. **Performance Report:**
   - Impressions (how many times you show up)
   - Clicks (how many people click)
   - Average position (what page you rank on)

2. **Coverage Report:**
   - How many pages are indexed
   - Should show 5 pages (or more as you add content)

3. **Top Queries:**
   - What keywords people find you for
   - Start with brand name, then long-tail

### Google Analytics (Check weekly)

Track:
- Organic Search traffic (should grow every month)
- Pages per session (shows engagement)
