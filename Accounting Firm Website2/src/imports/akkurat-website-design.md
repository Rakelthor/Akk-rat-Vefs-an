Design a modern, clean one-page website for a financial services company called Akkúrat.
The company provides bookkeeping, payroll, VAT reporting, and financial insight services for small and medium-sized businesses in Iceland.
The design should feel professional, precise, calm, and trustworthy, similar to modern Nordic financial brands. Avoid corporate banking style — keep it clean, minimal, and modern.
The site should look credible for a professional services firm but still approachable.
Target audience:
Small business owners, entrepreneurs, and managers who want reliable accounting services and better financial visibility.
Language: Icelandic.
Brand tone:
Precise, clear, trustworthy, modern.
Layout Structure
Create a single-page website with the following sections:
1. Hero Section
Headline:
"Akkúrat – Fjármál og rekstrarsýn fyrir fyrirtæki"
Subheadline explaining the value:
"Bókhald, launavinnsla og skýr yfirsýn sem hjálpar fyrirtækjum að halda betri stjórn á rekstrinum."
Include two call-to-action buttons:
Bóka kynningarsamtal
Sjá þjónustu
Include subtle financial or analytical design elements (lines, grid, data-inspired graphics).
2. Trust / Credibility Section
Display 3–4 credibility highlights:
M.Acc í reikningshaldi og endurskoðun
Reynslumikil í rekstri fyrirtækja
Persónuleg og fagleg þjónusta
Lausnir fyrir lítil og meðalstór fyrirtæki
Use simple icon cards.
3. Services Section
Create four service cards:
Bókhald
Laun og skil
Rekstraryfirlit
Stuðningur við eigendur
Each card should have a simple icon and short description.
4. Who We Help Section
Short section explaining typical clients:
ráðgjafar og þjónustufyrirtæki
verslun og netverslun
veitingastaðir
heilsu- og sérfræðiþjónusta
lítil og meðalstór fyrirtæki
Use a clean grid layout.
5. Pricing Section
Title:
"Verð og þjónustuleiðir"
Show three pricing tiers:
Grunnþjónusta – frá 60.000 kr / mánuði
Hefðbundin þjónusta – 80.000–150.000 kr / mánuði
Rekstrarsýn – frá 180.000 kr / mánuði
Use simple pricing cards.
Include note:
"Endanlegt verð ræðst af umfangi rekstrar og er ákveðið eftir stutt kynningarsamtal."
6. About Section
Short company description:
"Að baki Akkúrat er reynsla úr reikningshaldi, endurskoðun og rekstri fyrirtækja. Markmiðið er að veita fyrirtækjum áreiðanlega fjármálaþjónustu ásamt skýrari yfirsýn yfir stöðu og þróun rekstrarins."
7. Contact Section
Title:
"Hafðu samband"
Include:
email
phone
contact form
button: "Óska eftir samtali"
Visual Style
Style should follow Nordic design principles:
minimalist layout
lots of white space
modern sans-serif typography
soft shadows
subtle financial/data graphics
Color palette:
Primary: deep navy or dark slate
Secondary: soft grey
Accent: muted green or teal
Avoid bright colors.
UX
Mobile-first design.
Clear sections and smooth scrolling.
Professional but approachable tone.
Result
The page should feel like a modern financial advisory firm, not a traditional accounting office.
